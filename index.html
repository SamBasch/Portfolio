About Me

I'm Sam Baschnagel, a full-stack web developer

My journey to becoming a full-stack developer began during my high school years. After taking courses online, I decided that coding (in one shape or form) was the path I wanted to pursue. With that mindset, I went to college to obtain an associate's degree in Computer Engineering Technologies. A few years after college, I decided that full-stack software development was my path, and I attended CoderFoundry's Bootcamp. Now I am a certified full-stack developer ready to work with a team and take on challenging and helpful software projects.
